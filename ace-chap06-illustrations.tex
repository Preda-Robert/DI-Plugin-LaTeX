\chapter[Illustrations]{Illustrations, diagrams, and screenshot guide}
\label{chap6:illustrations}

This chapter lists \textbf{recommended figures} for the thesis. Placeholders appear in the PDF until you replace them with \verb|\includegraphics| (files under \texttt{DI-Plugin-LaTeX/img/thesis/} suggested). Each entry states \emph{what to capture}, \emph{where in the thesis it is referenced}, and \emph{why it helps the reader}.

\section{How to insert your assets}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item Export PNG or PDF at 300\,dpi; width 12--14\,cm in print.
	\item Save as \texttt{img/thesis/fig-NN-shortname.png}.
	\item Replace \verb|\thesisfigureplaceholder{...}| with:
\begin{verbatim}
\includegraphics[width=0.95\textwidth]{img/thesis/fig-01-architecture}
\end{verbatim}
	\item Keep \verb|\caption| and \verb|\label| unchanged so cross-references work.
\end{enumerate}
